what is gather.
✦

A different concept every month

Candle making one month, a sommelier the next, a therapist on boundaries after that. Always something worth leaving the house for.

✦

Real connection, not networking

20 to 30 women in a room who actually want to meet each other. Good drinks, good food, no awkward business card energy.

✦

Right here, not in Toronto

Everything you'd drive an hour for, happening 10 minutes from your house. Easy parking, no long commute, no babysitter math. Just show up.

how it works

Simple, by design.

01

We announce the concept

Each month we reveal a new theme a few weeks ahead. Anticipation is part of it.

02

You grab a ticket

Events are 20–30 women, intentionally kept small. Tickets usually $45–65. They go fast.

03

You show up

Evening or daytime, weekday or weekend — the format fits the concept. Workshop, drinks, food, conversation. That's it.

04

You leave with more than you came with

New skills, new people, new plans to come back. Usually all three.

about us

We started this because we needed it.

We're three women living in Burlington, doing all the things — careers, kids, the group chats we're always a day late to.

We had built a life here. What we hadn't built yet was a community. The kind where you actually know people nearby. Where someone texts you on a Tuesday just because. Where you leave a Wednesday night feeling more like yourself than when you walked in.

gather. is what we made for ourselves — and from what we're hearing, a lot of women across Burlington, Oakville, and Hamilton have been waiting for it too.
